Validation & Performance

Experimental results.

Performance validation of the Smart Compression Sleeve — synchronized sensor acquisition, biomechanical tracking, and machine-learning–based exercise form classification.

Sensor Validation

Three sensors, time-aligned.

Full-system synchronized validation demonstrating simultaneous acquisition of IMU, EMG, and PPG signals during bicep curl motion. The system maintained stable real-time data streaming and synchronized signal capture throughout testing — confirming that all three sensing modalities produce usable, time-aligned signal.

Synchronized IMU, EMG, and PPG sensor outputs collected during real-time exercise testing, demonstrating stable acquisition and full-circuit validation.
Motion Dynamics

IMU motion dynamics.

Relative elbow angle and forearm gyroscope velocity were extracted from synchronized upper-arm and forearm IMUs. Good-form repetitions produced smoother, more repeatable trajectories, while bad-form swinging trials showed irregular angle changes and sharper gyroscope velocity spikes. A 4 Hz Butterworth low-pass filter improved signal stability before feature extraction. These IMU-derived features are the inputs to the form classifier.

Good vs. bad form IMU motion dynamics. Raw and filtered relative-angle estimates and forearm gyroscope velocity. Good form shows smoother, repeatable motion; bad form shows the irregular patterns associated with swinging.
Classifier

Four classes, cleanly separated.

The form classifier is a Random Forest trained on per-rep features derived from the IMU signals (elbow-angle and gyroscope kinematics). EMG and PPG are captured and validated as activation and heart-rate signals, but are not used as classifier inputs. Four form categories are distinguished — Normal, Under-extension, Swinging, and Left-swinging — with GroupKFold validation by trial to prevent leakage and evaluate generalization across repetitions.

Confusion matrix for the Random Forest classifier, showing 96.6% ± 3.5% overall accuracy across four exercise form classes.
