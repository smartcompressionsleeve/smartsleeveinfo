Validation & Performance

Experimental results.

Performance validation of the Smart Compression Sleeve including synchronized sensor acquisition, biomechanical tracking, and machine learning–based exercise form classification.

Sensor Validation

Full-system synchronized validation demonstrating simultaneous acquisition of EMG, IMU, and PPG signals during bicep curl motion. The system maintained stable real-time data streaming and synchronized physiological signal capture throughout testing.

Synchronized EMG, IMU, and PPG sensor outputs collected during real-time exercise testing demonstrating stable acquisition and full-circuit validation.

Classifier Validation

Random Forest classification results for four exercise form categories: Normal, Under-extension, Swinging, and Left-swinging. GroupKFold validation was used to prevent trial leakage and evaluate generalization performance across repetitions.

Confusion matrix for the Random Forest classifier showing 96.6% ± 3.5% overall classification accuracy across four exercise form classes.
