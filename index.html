A wearable that knows
good form from bad.

A multi-sensor compression sleeve that classifies bicep curl form in real time — bringing objective, clinical-grade feedback into at-home rehabilitation.

See the results → Meet the team
Headline Result
96.6%

Four-class form classification accuracy across 417 labeled bicep curl repetitions, using five-fold trial-grouped cross-validation.
